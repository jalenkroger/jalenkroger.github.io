Programs & Classes

From junior camps to adaptive tennis and adult cardio.

Adult Tennis

We offer group lessons, private lessons, league opportunities and cardio tennis for adult players of all levels.

  • Beginner: Brand new to tennis. Learn basic strokes, skills and scoring, and an introduction to rallying.
  • Adult Ready to Rally: For players ready to get involved in rallies and learn match play basics.
  • Adult Drill Level 1 (3.0 NTRP): Fine-tune specific strokes, develop better shot selection, and learn match strategy.
  • Adult Drill Level 2 (3.5+ NTRP): Advanced drill group to produce better spin, exploit weaknesses, and learn match percentages.

Adult Leagues

  • Men's & Women's Doubles: Ranges from 3.0 up to 4.5 level leagues available.
  • Beginner Doubles League: Open to men and women looking for introductory match play.

Junior Tennis

Welcome to your one-stop hub for all things junior tennis at Woods and across Nebraska.

Free & Reduced Court Opportunities

We want players practicing outside of regular practice times!

  • Junior Groups Free "Day-Of": Play outdoors for free as long as there is court availability.
  • Private Lessons $5 "Day-Of": Play indoors or outdoors for $5 (limit one hour per lesson taken).

Adaptive & Wheelchair Tennis

Woods Tennis Center offers ongoing programming for players of all abilities depending on the time of year, in partnership with Madonna Rehabilitation Hospital.

  • Adaptive Red Ball: For younger players ages ~5-10, just beginning to pick up the game. Red balls are used.
  • Tennis Without Limits: Adaptive group for younger youth (ages ~6-15). Orange and green balls are used.
  • Tennis Buddies: Open to players ages 16+ interested in learning the game of tennis. Green and yellow balls are used.
  • Competitive Adult: An invited adaptive group for more experienced and nationally ranked players to refine their game.
  • Wheelchair Tennis: Wheelchair programming for junior and adult players.

For more information on specific programming and times, please reach out to Kaitlin or Kevin.

Tournaments & USTA

In-House Tournaments

We offer one-day in-house singles tournaments for players in our program (Red through Tournament classes). Players must be enrolled in the current session or have been in a session within the last six months. The format varies based on the number of players signed up.

Indoor Club Championships

Must have been in group classes, lessons, or leagues within the past year.
